Welcome to .:digitalalliancestudios.

We are a professional studio; offering room & equipment rentals, production classes and more!
.:DAS concentrates specifically on electronic music production and djing, but we work with other experts and vendors in the industry if you are looking for something specific. Whether you just need a couple hours... or a couple days we can accomodate your situation. We can also operate at hours other businesses are closed... so if you are a night owl and want an overnight session, we can do that too.
